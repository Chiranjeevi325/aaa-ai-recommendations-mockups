Student — Discover & Focus

1. Dashboard — Suggested for You

"Suggested for You" widget with mixed video / practice / chapter cards, plus a colour-coded Topic Mastery panel and a Weak Topics summary.

2. All Recommendations

Full page grouped by type — Watch Videos, Practice Questions, Focus Chapters. Max 5 per type, adaptive difficulty, each card dismissible with "×".

3. Topic Mastery Breakdown

Tap a topic to see how mastery is calculated (test scores + video + chapter tests), time spent, accuracy by difficulty, and what to do next.
1 screen

Smart Practice — Adaptive Tests

4. Smart Test (AI question selection)

Build a custom test where the AI picks questions from your past performance and adapts difficulty in real time as you answer.
1 screen

After a Test — Act Immediately

5. Post-Test Recommendations

On the results page — targeted next steps based on the topics the student performed poorly on, so they can fix gaps while it's fresh.
2 screens

Access & Activation

6. Upgrade Prompt (Free / Standard)

Recommendations are exclusive to the Advanced tier. Free / Standard students see a locked preview with an invitation to upgrade.

7. Activation — Minimum 5 Tests

Advanced students need 5 completed tests before recommendations activate — shown as a progress state until they qualify.
